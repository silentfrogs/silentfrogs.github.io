Programming Test

You are tasked to create a program to allow customers to purchase items from an electronics store

  1. Store the data (10 Points)
  2. Allow the Customer to pick
    1. A Phone (3 points)
    2. A Tablet (3 points)
    3. SIM Card (4 points)
  3. Output total costs (10 Points)
  4. Allow customers to purchase more than one item of its class (example : more than 1 phone) (10 Points)
  5. For each item after 2 items a customer adds to their cart , there will be a 10% discount on the total price (10 Points)
    • 1 item = 100% , 2 item = 100% , 3 item = 90% , 4 item = 81% , 5 item = 72.9% ...
